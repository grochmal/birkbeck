Appendix F: Resources on Creative Commons Attribution-ShareAlike 3.0 and other
GNU GPL compatible licenses

The license under which this work is delivered can be found under
http://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/3.0, although the full details about
this license and other similar ones can be found at http://creativecommons.org
A discussion on the purpose of the creative commons licenses is available at
http://creativecommons.org/about

Another good discussion about copyleft licenses is on
https://www.gnu.org/licenses/licenses.html, and the list of currently
recognized copyleft licenses at: https://www.gnu.org/licenses/license-list.html
Finally a discussion about the reason that such copyright (copyleft) licenses
are beneficial for the eduction system can be found under
http://www.gnu.org/education/education.html

References: (GNU, 2013) and (CC, 2013)
